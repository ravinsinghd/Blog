More no of people like a puzzle game,normally the puzzle games are contain some pictures then you will arrange it .but try differently in vlc player .you will play any video in vlc player then follow the below steps to play a video puzzle in vlc

1)open any video in vlc
2)go to tools in menu bar
3)goto effect and filters
4)Then video effect
5)Under that goto geometry
6)then one check box is puzzle game
7)now your video will be change as video puzzle

you can specify no of rows and columns are also you can specify.under that some image will attached for step by step
