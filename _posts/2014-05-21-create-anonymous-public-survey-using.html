Many of you know about Google forms powered by Google Drive. This option is use full to collect the survey from the public users.Now Microsoft also  introduced this option in OneDrive and OneDrive for business (Formally skydrive & Skydrive Pro).
By using this you can create your own survey and send it to the users and you can collect the opinion from the public users.
If you are having the SharePoint subscription please go to your OneDrive for business or else login OneDrive site with your Microsoft account

Go To your One Drive or One Drive for Business
One Drive One Drive for Business

After this One Excel sheet will open in Web app with form editing panel

You can edit the questions by clicking the gear symbol on the top right corner

Once finish your editing then you can save and preview the form.

Then you can share the form to any of public user by clicking Share Survey.
